Goals
Our core goals are to revolutionize the availability and accessibility of large-scale data-intensive science to the scientific community. In order to achieve these goals, we aim to leverage our long history with SDSS to consolidate, re-engineer and extend the SkyServer building blocks so as to provide the same unique capabilities across  the scientific spectrum.
